Insecurity

Catch Phone Snoopers Red Handed!

iOS app that snaps a quick photo when someone tries to unlock your phone without permission. This quirky app lets friends and family know when their loved ones have been taking a peek while they're away from their phone. Images are not stored into the camera roll, so the snooper has no way of deleting the photo after they've been caught. Insecurity has been getting hundreds of downloads a day simply due to word of mouth!

  • Thousands of active users after being in app store for only 1 month
  • UIImagePicker & NSNotifcationCenter to capture photos
  • Parse database to handle profiles

Cones

Ice Cream Truck Tracker

This is an iOS app that tracks local ice cream trucks via GPS and places them on a map near your current location. This is a fun way to find delicious ice cream! In order to achieve this we implemented the following technologies:

  • Apple MapKit to display GPS coordinates.
  • Parse to store and deliver ice cream truck coordinates.
  • CLLocation manager to receive and transmit user and ice cream truck driver locations.
